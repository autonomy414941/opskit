OpsKit FAQ

Which calculator should I start with?
Start with SLA Downtime for target-setting, then use Error Budget to track whether the target is sustainable.

How accurate is incident cost modeling?
It is directional. Use it to compare scenarios and improve prioritization, not to produce final accounting numbers.

How often should we recalculate on-call load?
Most teams should refresh it after rota changes, alerting policy shifts, or major product launches.
